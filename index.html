Our Objective:

The primary goal of this website is to enable families in conflict zones or during natural disasters to find one another in the event of a tragic separation. We then aggregate this in-depth data in order to provide statistics on refugee movements and vaccination initiatives for NGOs and local authorities.

What Makes Our Project Special:

Current applications built for NGOs are designed for pure functionality, whereas the services provided for refugees are often separate entities that, though they offer useful information, often fail to harness the communication abilities of the people they are trying to help. By combining an emergency communication network with modern “big data” techniques, we can greatly augment the quality and quantity of critical data.
